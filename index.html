Overview

E4RS is an attempt at making the road safer for everyone. E4RS locates, identifies, and displays typical noises on the road (car horn, emergency vehicle sirens). This allows deaf, hard of hearing, and distracted drivers/riders to have a comparable amount of safety on the road as everyone else. Since motor vehicle related injuries are one of the leading causes of preventable deaths, this project focuses on making the road safer.
